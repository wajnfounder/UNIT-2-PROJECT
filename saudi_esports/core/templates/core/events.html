About This Event

Gamers8 2026 — Beyond the Horizon — is set to be the defining moment in esports history. For the first time, the festival spans two iconic venues: Boulevard Riyadh City and the newly completed NEOM Arena. With a staggering $80M prize pool across 10 titles and 200 competing teams from around the globe, this edition cements Saudi Arabia as the permanent capital of world esports.

EVENT HISTORY

The Rise of Saudi Esports Events

2017

Saudi Esports Federation Founded

The Kingdom establishes its official esports governing body, marking the start of a new era.

2019

First National Esports League

Saudi Arabia launches its first structured national esports league with 32 teams across 3 titles.

2022

Gamers8 Inaugural Edition

The world's largest esports festival launches in Riyadh with a $15M prize pool and 6 titles.

2023

Gamers8 2nd Edition — $35M

Saudi Arabia doubles down with a $35M prize pool, cementing its status as the global esports capital.

2024

Gamers8 3rd Edition — $45M

A record-breaking $45M across 8 titles. Saudi Arabia hosts VCT Lock//In, Riyadh Masters, and more.

2025

Gamers8 4th Edition — $60M

The future begins. A $60M prize pool, 9 titles, and 160 teams from around the world.
